FAMOUS AUTHORS

    2.       
Ernest Miller Hemingway was an American novelist, short-story writer,
journalist, and sportsman. His economical and understated style—which
he termed the iceberg theory—had a strong influence on 20th-century
fiction, while his adventurous lifestyle and his public image brought
him admirationfrom later generations.

    3.       
Joanne Rowling is a British author and philanthropist known by her pen
name J. K. Rowling. She wrote a seven-volume children's fantasy series,
Harry Potter, published from 1997 to 2007.

    4.       
Rabindranath Tagore FRAS was a Bengali polymath—poet, writer, playwright,
composer, philosopher, social reformer and painter. He reshaped Bengali
literature and music as well as Indian art with Contextual Modernism in
the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
